I'm used to being confronted in both complex and creative environments.

The products I work on must allow users to switch between multiple themes, designed according to their ages, abilities, and profiles. Beside making applications simple, easy-to-use and visually appealing, I especially pay attention to the consistency of the multiple aspects of a same product, whether it's about the user experience or expressing the brand identity through strong ideas.

I used to maintain and extend a CSS framework, build smart components and ensure a consistent architecture.
