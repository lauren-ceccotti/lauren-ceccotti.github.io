--- title: About ref: about description: About my experience. ---

I started to be interested in combining digital technologies with an artistic process when I was studying in École des beaux-arts. I made some personal projects while I started creating online portfolios for artists or art galleries websites as a freelancer which made me somebody autonomous and with a high sense of responsibilities .
These collaborations made me want to improve my skills, so I completed a developer professional training and got my IT degree. Then I was hired in companies providing BtoB tools for luxury groups, education, administration… and also institutional or BtoC websites.
I made many teammates, and even if I still take some freelance missions, I prefer being a part of a team that works on long term projects.

Back from an eight months trip around the world, I’m looking for a team with whom I wish to take up inspiring challenges and build ambitious projects.

If you’re looking for a Designer who understands software development, creates bridges between design and technology and can upgrade your product to the next level, then we should talk together !
